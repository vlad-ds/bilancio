Metric Explanations

Total dues S_t
Sum of payment obligations maturing on day t.
Min net liquidity ̅M_t
Minimum system net liquidity needed if obligations are perfectly offset; equals sum of debtor shortfalls max(0, F_i - I_i).
Start-of-day money M_t
System means-of-payment available at the start of day t (cash, deposits, reserves).
Liquidity gap G_t
Shortfall at the start of the day: max(0, ̅M_t - M_t).
Netting potential α_t
Share of dues that can be cleared by circulation/netting: 1 - ̅M_t / S_t.
Operational peak M^peak_t
Peak amount of money simultaneously out in circuit when replaying realized settlement sequence.
Gross settled
Total value actually settled on day t.
Intraday velocity v_t
How intensively money is reused intraday: gross settled / operational peak.
On-time settlement φ_t
Share of dues with due_day=t that settle on day t.
Deferral/default δ_t
1 - φ_t (portion not settled on time; may reflect deferrals or defaults).
Creditor HHI^+
Concentration of net creditor positions among those with n_i > 0.
Debtor shortfall shares DS_t(i)
Each net debtor's share of ̅M_t, indicating who needs liquidity.
